Introduction

Research Problems


1. The 21st century has witnessed an escalation in the challenges associated with maintaining clean and safe water sources, as highlighted by Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 6, which emphasizes the crucial need for ensuring universal access to clean water and sanitation.

2. Qatar faces a critical challenge concerning water quality and scarcity, exacerbated by rapid urbanization, population growth, and environmental stressors.

3. Moreover, the country grapples with the disposal of Date Palm waste, a byproduct of its thriving agricultural industry. This waste presents both an environmental concern and an untapped resource potential (Hussain et al., 2015).

Research Questions

The objective of this study is to make a Water Quality Management Device out of Date Pits and Raspberry Pi Pico. Specifically, this research aims to answer the following questions:

1. How effective is the Water Quality Management Device in terms of contaminant removal?

2. What is the accuracy of the Water Quality Management Device in assessing the water quality in terms of pH level?

3. What is the accuracy of the Water Quality Management Device in assessing the water quality in terms of turbidity?

4. What is the accuracy of the Water Quality Management Device in assessing the water quality in terms of total dissolved solids?

Research Hypothesis

H1: It is feasible to make a Water Quality Management Device out of Date Pits and Raspberry Pi Pico.
